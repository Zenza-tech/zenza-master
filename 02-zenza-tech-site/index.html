Our Story

Zenza Technology exists to build software that solves real problems for African markets — starting with two very different ones. Zenza FID gives banks and financial institutions a shared memory for fraud. ZenVest gives everyday people one place to access credit, build an investment portfolio, and move between the two.

Different problems, different users, different regulatory worlds — but the same underlying philosophy runs through both: don't replace the institutions that already do this well, sit alongside them, and make the whole experience smarter, safer, and easier to understand for the person actually using it.

"Innovate to Solve Problems."

That's not just a tagline — it's the filter every product idea goes through before it becomes a Zenza Technology product. Each one gets its own team, its own hiring, its own plan, because each one requires genuinely different expertise — but every one of them ships under the same standard.
