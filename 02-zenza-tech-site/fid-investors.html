01 · The Opportunity

Every bank has fraud knowledge. Almost none of it is governed, shared, or reused.

Intelligence lives in spreadsheets, inboxes and individual memory — not in a system a rules engine, an AI model, or a regulator can rely on.

20+Commercial banks in Nigeria alone, before MFBs, PSBs and fintechs
0Purpose-built fraud intelligence layer most institutions run today
30%+Target reduction in case resolution time with a governed link-analysis view
4Core banking systems we integrate with directly (Finacle, Flexcube, Temenos, BANKS/ICSFS)

Transaction-monitoring engines like Clari5 are excellent at scoring a transaction against a rule in real time. What they were never designed to be is a system of record — a single, governed, continuously enriched repository of who the bad actors are, how they're connected, and what's already been learned about them.

Zenza FID fills that gap with software: a fraud-intelligence repository, watchlist management, identity resolution, relationship analytics, and case management — sitting alongside existing fraud engines, not competing with them. Lower switching cost. Faster sales cycle.
