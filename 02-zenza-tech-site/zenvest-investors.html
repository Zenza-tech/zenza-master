The Opportunity

Three growing markets, none of them connected for the end user.

Nigeria's digital lending market, retail investment market, and fractional/proptech real estate market are each growing independently — no platform has credibly combined them into one trusted experience.

A large, digitally native, underserved Gen Z and young professional population is actively looking for accessible ways to build wealth — evidenced by the growth of category leaders like Bamboo, Risevest, Cowrywise, and Chaka, each proving demand in one lane of what ZenVest combines into one.

ZenVest's addressable opportunity is the union of users who want more than one of these — which, given how interconnected financial decisions actually are, is most of the target market, not a niche within it.

The Model

ZenVest doesn't originate credit, hold property, or manage funds.

It's the orchestration layer — the AI risk engine, the unified dashboard, and the financial literacy foundation — connecting users to an open set of licensed partners.

Pillar 01

Credit

Loans, credit facilities, bill payment — licensed credit partners, AI-enhanced risk scoring.

Pillar 02

Investment

Real estate, ETFs, stablecoins, bonds, shares — licensed investment partners, suitability-matched.

Pillar 03

Real Estate

Sourcing, brokerage, management — turned into fractional, accessible co-ownership.

The Bridge — Deliberately the Last Feature, Not the First

Credit and investment each launch and prove themselves independently before any bridge between them activates — with asset-class-specific loan-to-value limits grounded in real performance data, never a single blanket ratio. Real estate isn't eligible as credit collateral until a proper secondary-transfer mechanism exists.

Why This Is Defensible
Network Effects

Three pillars, one user

A user active across credit, investment, and real estate is significantly harder for a single-category competitor to win away.

Data Advantage

The AI layer compounds

Risk models improve with usage across all three pillars simultaneously — a moat a credit-only or investment-only competitor can't replicate.

Open Ecosystem

No single point of failure

If one partner underperforms or exits, others can be onboarded without rebuilding the platform.

The Ask

Sized to reach a live-revenue MVP, honestly.

Full financial detail — funding stage, amount, and use of funds — is being developed alongside a proper financial model as Phase 0 partner conversations progress, so assumptions are grounded in real partner terms rather than estimated ahead of them.

What can be said now: ZenVest is designed to show investors a platform with live, diversified, multi-pillar fee revenue from its MVP stage — the funding ask will be sized to reach that milestone, plus 12–18 months of runway to prove the model with real partner and user data.
