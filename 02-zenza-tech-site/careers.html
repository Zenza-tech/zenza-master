How We Pay

Every product at Zenza Technology is early-stage, and every offer reflects that honestly.

Cash compensation is modest at this stage across the company — closer to a stipend than a market-rate salary for most roles — paired with meaningful equity and real ownership of what you build. This applies whether you're joining a company-wide function or a specific product team; each product's careers page has more detail on what that looks like there.
