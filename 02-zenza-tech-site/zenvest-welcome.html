Our Story

Financial opportunity in Nigeria is scattered across systems that don't talk to each other. Credit sits with lenders. Investment sits with asset managers. Real estate sits with brokers you probably don't have a relationship with. A whole generation of financially capable people — Gen Z especially — stays underserved not because they don't want to build wealth, but because no one has made it as easy, immediate, and well-explained as spending.

We started ZenVest to close that gap. Not by becoming a bank, or a broker, or a real estate company ourselves — by connecting you to the licensed experts who already do those things well, in one place, with one identity, protected by the same guardrails whether you're borrowing, investing, or both.

"Create wealth together." — What ZenVest actually means

Together with licensed partners who know credit, investment, and real estate better than any single app could on its own. Together with a community of people building wealth at the same time you are. And together with a platform that's honest about what it takes to do this safely — not just what sounds exciting.

Why It Matters

Credit, investment, and real estate — as one connected financial life, not three separate apps.

Most platforms make you choose a lane. ZenVest is the only one built around the idea that your credit, your investments, and your future all belong in the same picture.

Credit

Access when you need it

Salary-backed loans, credit facilities, and bill payment — from licensed credit partners, underwritten with the help of a fuller financial picture than a lender usually sees.

Investment

Grow what you have

Real estate co-ownership, ETFs, bonds, shares, and stable-value assets — matched to your goals and risk appetite, explained in plain language before you commit to anything.

The Bridge

Move between the two, safely

Use credit to invest when it makes sense. Use investment returns to responsibly settle credit. Guardrails — not guesswork — decide how far that can go.

How We Protect You

The riskiest feature is the one we're most careful with.

Letting you invest with credit and settle credit from investments is powerful — and it's exactly the kind of feature that hurts people when it's rushed. That's why credit and investment each prove themselves on their own first, with real data, before any bridge between them goes live — and even then, within limits set per asset type, not one blanket rule for everything.
