Our Story

Every fraud team has felt this: you finally catch someone, close the case, and know — with total certainty — they'll simply try again somewhere else. A different bank. A different branch. Sometimes even a different name, on the same person.

The information that could stop this already exists. It's just stuck — in a spreadsheet on one person's laptop, in an email nobody else can search, in the memory of an investigator who might move on next year and take it with them.

Zenza FID gives that knowledge a permanent home. Not by asking banks to hand over their customers' data to us or to each other — that's not something any institution should have to accept. Instead, we built a way for banks to quietly confirm "have you seen this person before?" without either side ever seeing the other's private records.

"A fraudster doesn't stop at a bank's front door. Our defenses shouldn't either." — Why we started Zenza FID

That's the whole idea. One bank's hard-won experience becomes protection for every institution connected to the network — instantly, safely, and without anyone's personal information ever changing hands.

What We Do

In plain terms, here's what Zenza FID gives a bank.

We sit alongside the tools a bank already uses — we don't replace them.

A single record of every known bad actor

One profile per person, no matter how many names, accounts or channels they've used.

A watch-list that actually stays current

Every flag is checked, tracked, and removed properly, so nothing gets missed or forgotten.

Cases that move, not stall

A way to check without asking

How We Start

We show you what you've been missing, before you change anything.

We start with a bank's own past data, offline — no connection to any live system, nothing for IT security to worry about. From that alone, we put together a short report showing the connections and repeat offenders their current tools missed. They see the value before committing to anything.
