How We Pay

We're a small, pre-seed company. We can't pay handsomely yet — and we'd rather tell you that now than have you find out later.

What we can offer is real ownership: meaningful equity, a founding-team seat at the table, and hands-on experience that's hard to get once a company is 200 people. Cash compensation today is modest — closer to a stipend than a market-rate salary for most roles — and will move toward market rate as we close funding and land paying pilot institutions.

If you need full market-rate pay right now, we'll likely not be a fit yet, and that's completely fine. If you're drawn to building something from zero with a small, accountable team, this is exactly the stage for that.

Cash

Modest monthly stipend for full-time roles, reviewed as funding lands. Interns: stipend where budget allows.

Equity

Meaningful founding-team equity for early full-time hires, formalized once the company is incorporated.

Growth

Direct mentorship, real ownership of shipped work, and a reference built on outcomes an investor can see.

Vision

The trusted fraud-intelligence layer for African financial institutions

Zenza FID centralizes fraud intelligence — watchlists, bad-actor identifiers, entity relationships, case management and analytics — into a single governed layer that sits alongside a bank's existing transaction-monitoring engine, rather than replacing it.

The Problem

Knowledge that should protect an institution instead sits in spreadsheets

When a bad actor is caught in one department, branch or bank, that knowledge rarely reaches every other place they could strike again. Existing fraud engines score transactions well; they were never built to be a governed system of record for who the bad actors are and how they're connected.

What You'd Be Building
  • Fraud Intelligence Repository — governed, searchable store of bad-actor profiles and identifiers
  • Watchlist Management — maker-checker-approved flagging with real-time sync
  • Rule Trigger Engine — configurable detection rules across transactions and entities
  • Relationship / Link Analytics — graph-based views connecting entities and cases
  • Case Management — SLA-driven investigation workflow with escalation
  • Containment & Compliance Controls — time-boxed, auditable restrictions bounded by a hard 72-hour/court-order limit
  • Cross-Bank Hashed Intelligence Network — privacy-preserving matching across institutions using cryptographic hashes, never raw customer data
  • API Integration Layer — connectors for Finacle, Flexcube, Temenos, BANKS/ICSFS and NIBSS/BVN services
Indicative Stack

Node.js / Java microservices on Kubernetes, React frontend, PostgreSQL + a graph database (e.g. Neo4j) for relationship analytics, Kafka for real-time event streaming, Python for the ML risk-scoring pipeline, HSM/KMS-backed encryption throughout.

Where We Are

Pre-seed, pre-MVP. Phase 1 (0–5 months) is the Starter tier: repository, watchlists, RBAC and audit trail, integrated with one pilot bank. That's the build the first engineering hires join.

What We Won't Compromise On

Security and compliance are not an afterthought. ISO 27001-aligned controls from day one, maker-checker on every high-risk action, and a strict boundary around autonomous account actions.
